Most Haunted Historic Locations in Virginia

The places marked on the map are reportedly haunted locations in the state of Virginia. Many of the locations have been converted into historic landmarks. Some of the reasons why the location is deemed to be haunted is due to murders, curses and death from natural and unnatural causes. Vistors of these locations report being touched, seeing ghosts or hearing noises when nobody is around. If one wants to scare themselves and learn about history at the same time, visit one of these sites.
